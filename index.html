Education Hub
KNOW YOUR ENEMY

Learn to spot every type of scam. Click any card for full red flags, warning signs, and what to do. Share this page — awareness is the #1 weapon against fraud.

💔
Romance Scam
Fake relationships built online to extract money. Stolen photos, elaborate backstories, urgent emergencies.
Global losses: $1.3B/year
📈
Investment / Crypto Fraud
Promises of high returns on fake platforms. Money deposited, platform disappears with everything.
Global losses: $5.6B/year
🎣
Phishing
Fake emails, SMS or calls pretending to be your bank, SARS, SASSA or known brands to steal credentials.
Global losses: $3.4B/year
✉️
Advance Fee (419) Fraud
Promise of millions if you pay a small fee. Fees keep growing. The money never arrives.
Global losses: $700M/year
💼
Job / Employment Scam
Fake job offers requiring upfront payment for training, registration or equipment before starting.
Global losses: $200M/year
💻
Tech Support Scam
Fake Microsoft or Apple alerts claiming your device is infected. Remote access given, money stolen.
Global losses: $800M/year
🎱
Lottery / Prize Scam
You won a prize you never entered. Pay fees to claim. The prize never materialises.
Global losses: $120M/year
🔺
Pyramid / Ponzi Scheme
Early investors paid from new recruits' money. Collapses when recruitment slows leaving most with losses.
Global losses: $2.9B/year
